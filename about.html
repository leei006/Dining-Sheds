An archive of spaces that gave restaurateurs peace amidst the pandemic. The dining sheds recorded in this book are no longer a part of New York City. The wooden sheds are at rest in these pages.

NYC GOV: Dining Out Guidelines

DINING OUT

The simple truth is once something is gone you can't bring it back. The sheds that once kept us from the cold or gave us an intimate space away from the streets are a thing of the past. Whether you considered them a sore sight or a nuisance, the sheds impacted people for the better at one point. Did we forget that not long ago COVID kept us 6 feet apart. The sheds gave us a moment of escape.
